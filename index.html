About

Hey There!

I'm a full stack web developer who uses creativity and logic to create web sites and applications.

I am currently based in Atlanta, Georgia, where I attended General Assembly and completed a 12 week web development immersive course.

My facination for technology began early in high school, when my friend and I put together my family desktop computer out of parts we purchased online. After learning about all the components and how to build a computer, I decided to enroll in web design classes to put my machine to work.

A lot has changed since then, and I truly enjoy seeing all the new features and technologies being developed and am eager to learn of much as possible.

Some other interests of mine include culinary arts, swimming, playing music and, of course, learning!
